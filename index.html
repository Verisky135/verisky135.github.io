KUYJALAN

  • ABOUT
  • DETAILS

There are many apps or games that encourge its users to wander around on public area by using GPS. But not so many of them motivate its users to fully utilize the function of public areas. KuyJalan exist to help people to enjoy their games while making public areas functionate like it is supposed to.

Demo video (Youtube)

KuyJalan is an integrated game running on three platform : Unity, Android, and Arduino.

Players using their Android as a remote control, account management (register and login), and location management. Unity provide many different games based on users location. Arduino reads users RFID card to identified user's account.
